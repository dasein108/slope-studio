Slope Studio — Privacy Policy

Last updated: 7 June 2026

Slope Studio ("the Software") is a personal, self-hosted command-line tool run by an individual operator on their own machine. This policy explains what data it handles.

1. Who runs it

The Software is operated by the individual who installs it. There is no central server, no hosted service, and no third party that receives your data through us.

2. What data is processed

  • Authorization tokens. When you connect a platform account (e.g. TikTok) via its official OAuth flow, the resulting access and refresh tokens are stored locally on the operator's own machine so the Software can upload on your behalf. They are not transmitted to us or to any third party.
  • Video files and their metadata (titles, descriptions, tags) that you generate are stored locally and sent only to the platform you choose to publish to, using that platform's official API.
  • Public performance metrics (e.g. view and like counts of your own videos) may be fetched from a connected platform's API to help you decide what to publish, and are stored locally.

3. What we do NOT do

We do not collect, sell, share, or transmit your personal data to any third party. We do not operate analytics on you. All processing happens locally on the operator's machine.

4. Platform data

Data accessed through the TikTok API is used solely to upload the operator's own content and read the operator's own public stats, in line with TikTok's developer policies. You can revoke the Software's access at any time in your TikTok account settings.

5. Data retention & deletion

Locally stored tokens and files remain until the operator deletes them. Deleting the local token files (e.g. tiktok_token*.json) removes stored credentials.

6. Contact

Questions or deletion requests: acidpictures@gmail.com
